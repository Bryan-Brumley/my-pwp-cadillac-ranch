Purpose

The Cadillac Ranch PWP would be a landing page for the world famous tourist attraction outside ofd Amarillo, Texas. A place for potential guests to find our hours of business, events, and advertise new products. As well as photos of the attraction and directions.

Audience

For the most part the site will be used by people traveling down I-40 or old Route 66. The site will be designed for potential guests to be able to get directions, hours of operation, event calendar, and directions. Also it will be a gallery showcasing all the photos I have taken of the location for visitors to see.

Goal

The goal of this site is to provide a landing page for the Cadillac Ranch. It will also serve as a gallery for my Cadillac Ranch based photography. With embedded links it will also help the tourist attraction's social media presence.
